# Multiple Choice Questions

## Question 1
What is the main theme explored in Anton Chekhov's play "The Proposal"?

A. The dangers of greed and ambition  
B. The tendency of wealthy families to seek economic gain through marriage  
C. The importance of true love in marriage  
D. The traditions of Russian wedding ceremonies  

**Answer:** B  
**Explanation:** The play focuses on how wealthy families pursue marriages that benefit their estates and economic interests, not just emotional connections.

## Question 2
Which of the following best describes the initial misunderstanding between Lomov and Chubukov when Lomov arrives?

A. Chubukov thinks Lomov is there to borrow money  
B. Chubukov believes Lomov wants to purchase land  
C. Chubukov assumes Lomov is there to quarrel  
D. Chubukov expects Lomov to invite him to a party  

**Answer:** A  
**Explanation:** Chubukov suspects Lomov’s visit is to request a loan, as shown by his aside.

## Question 3

## Question 12
