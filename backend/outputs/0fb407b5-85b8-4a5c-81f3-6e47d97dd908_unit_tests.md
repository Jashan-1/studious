## Question 1
What are the primary preparations involved in a Russian wedding as described in the content?

**Model Answer:**
The primary preparations for a Russian wedding include arranging for rings, the bride's dress, cars for the wedding procession, and organizing a reception. Traditionally, the bride's family would cover the reception costs, but currently, both families usually share these expenses.

**Key Points:**
- Arranging rings and the bride's dress
- Organizing cars for the procession
- Shared expenses between families for the reception

## Question 4
What is the significance of the wedding toast in a Russian wedding, and how is it typically performed?

**Model Answer:**
The wedding toast is a significant tradition in Russian weddings, where a close friend or relative of the couple offers wishes for their happiness. After the toast, everyone raises their glasses and drinks together, symbolizing unity and celebration of the couple's new life.

**Key Points:**
- A friend or relative gives well wishes
- Everyone drinks together after the toast
- Symbolizes unity and celebration
